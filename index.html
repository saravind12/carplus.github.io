Design

The Problem

-65% of people asked in a survey said that they drive a car built before 2010
-Cars manufactured before 2010 lack modern safety features
-Modern safety features netted a 30% decrease in traffic casualties
-The most common collision was a rear end, easily preventable with a detection system

The Solution

A one-stop service that will provide customers with:
-Automatic Braking
-Blind Spot/Collision Alert
-Lane Departure Warning
-in their current cars.

Proof of Concept

-RC car 1/36 scale of normal car retrofitted with sensors, radar unit, and central computer
-Car successfully demonstrated the concept of autonomous braking and obstacle avoidance to potential customers

System Design

-Car+ safety system that uses a variety of sensors in combination with in-house designed proprietary software to maximize safety and minimize risk
-Parts for the safety system are readily available from a variety of vendors
